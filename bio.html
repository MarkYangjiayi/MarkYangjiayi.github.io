Short Bio

Jiayi Yang is a current undergraduate student of Telecommunications Engineering at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications(BUPT).

From July 2018 to present, he is a research intern at Center for Brain Inspired Computing Research, Tsinghua University advised by Prof. Guoqi Li.

His research interests are Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Brain Inspired Computing.
